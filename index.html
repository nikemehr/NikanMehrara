My Story

When I was 13, I remember seeing Google’s landing page for the first time and wondering how such a simple interface could power one of the most valuable companies in the world.

What fascinated me was not only the technology itself, but the business logic behind it. That curiosity gradually evolved into a deeper interest in how data, user behavior, and technology come together to create scalable products and meaningful business impact.

Today, that same curiosity drives my work in business intelligence, analytics, and machine learning.

Highlights

🗣️ Business Storytelling

I believe storytelling serves two critical purposes in business: explaining the present and shaping the future. It helps leaders frame current realities and challenges in a way that builds clarity and shared understanding. At the same time, it allows them to articulate a compelling vision of the future that aligns minds, inspires teams, and drives coordinated action.

🛠️ Technical Versatility

While I don’t see myself as purely technical, I continuously expand my skills in data science and analytical tools so I can operate technically when needed. Being technically versatile means moving comfortably between business questions and tools like SQL, Python, and BI platforms to turn data into practical insights and decisions.

🧩 Strategy and Systems Thinking

I’m naturally drawn to strategic and systems thinking because I enjoy understanding how different pieces of a business connect and influence each other. Rather than looking at problems in isolation, I like stepping back to see the broader structure, identify root drivers, and think about how decisions today shape outcomes over time.
